Why Choose Galileo Lifelong Learning Center?

Galileo Lifelong Learning Centre plays an active role in developing the Erasmus projects’ overall strategy and in designing and implementing education policies. GLL Centre is open to many individuals and organisations, although eligibility varies from one action to another and from one country to another. However, it provides opportunities almost for everyone in the field of education; teachers, trainers, students, doctors, professors, universities and businesses. The aim of GLL Centre is to contribute to the Europe 2020 strategy for growth, jobs, social equity and inclusion, as well as the aims of ET2020, the EU's strategic framework for education and training. GLL Centre also aims to promote the sustainable development of its partners in the field of higher education, and contribute to achieving the objectives of the EU Youth Strategy. The purpose of the GLL Centre is to find solutions about the topic below:

  • Increasing the sustainable lifelong learning education programs.
  • Sustainable clean environmental and global warming.
  • Encouraging young people to take part in European democracy.
  • Supporting innovation, cooperation and reform.
  • Reducing unemployment, especially among young people.
  • Promoting adult learning, especially for new skills and skills required by the labour market.
  • Reducing early school leaving.
  • Promoting cooperation and mobility with the EU's partner countries.
