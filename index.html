About Us

GALILEO LIFELONG LEARNING CENTER

offers numerous development opportunities for students, university employees and many other groups from around the world, who dream of science beyond their country. Training system abroad, in addition to increasing professional perspectives and experience, gives the opportunity to get independence, get to know new cultures and improve foreign languages. Life in Poland

Poland – a country which resides on what was formerly known as Eastern Europe – is a country whose tourist star is dramatically rising. A place which has long been steeped in tradition and history, it’s also a land of a land of considerable natural beauty with whose idyllic lakes, beaches and mountains which are perfectly juxtaposed to the cultural rigours of the historic cities. It has a very rich culture especially with its historical churches and museums. Poland, a country that has seen hundreds of wars throughout its history, completely erased from the map, and has undergone various resistances, has overcome all these difficulties and has been able to bring its values to this day. We can say that the stability and economy of the country are almost not affected by some crises happening around the world. Therefore, it receives serious investments, especially from other countries of Europe.
