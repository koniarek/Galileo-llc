Cetniewo Campsite( Władysławowo , Pomerania). It is one of the most important places that we take part! Central Sport Centre – Olympic Preparation Centre ,,Cetniewo” in Władysławowo is situated in the natural park-forest complex, on the high coast of the Baltic Sea. In 1952 Feliks Stamm, the tutor and coach of many Polish boxing fames, discovered this picturesque place for sport. Cetniewo Olympic Centre is currently a specialised centre for training boxing, wrestling, judo, fencing, weight lifting, team games: volleyball, basketball, handball, badminton, sport acrobatics, fights of the East. The centre offers its guests a hotel-restaurant service, with a full professional equipment, biological regeneration pavilion. The complex makes possible organising sportsmen groupings before important sport events (olympic games, World and Europe Championships), organising of the national and international tournaments, holidays during a whole year and for various periods, recreation and season staying. As You can see, Cetniewo Olympic Centre is the place which can fulfil your expectations. Spending time with young athletes from many parts of Europe has always made us happy!
