About Us

Galileo Lifelong Learning Centre plays an active role in developing the Erasmus projects’ overall strategy and in designing and implementing education policies. GLL Center is open to many individuals and organisations, although eligibility varies from one action to another and from one country to another. However, it provides opportunities almost for everyone in the field of education; teachers, trainers, students, doctors, professors, universities and businesses.

Marcin Małkiewicz

I am a graduate of Tourism and Recreation with a specialisation in Organisation of Tourist Enterprises at the Academy of Physical Education in Wrocław. From childhood, I was reading mythologies and books about ancient religions, I was interested in many cultures and beliefs, especially the ancient ones. At the same time, I was interested in the countries of the Near and Far East. Traveling around them was one of my first dreams that I managed to make a few years ago. On a daily basis, I run a company that deals with all types of work at height. Moreover, I am a Snowboard Instructor (SITS) and an international Kitesurfing Instructor (IKO). I have been practicing many other sports for over a decade: skiing, squash, yoga, frisbee ultimate, cycling and swimming. I also love cooking, which is also related to the journeys that I go on to look for and learn new flavours. It gives me great pleasure to take on new challenges and face them. As a result, each day is a new step forward. The moments spent on the road and learning about new world views give me a lot of energy and inspiration to act and implement new ideas. Seeing the world around me outside the box and noticing connections that many would not have thought of is one of my unconditional behaviours. That is why, together with my partner Alp, I decided to set up our company Galileo Lifelong Learning Center. The contacts I have made throughout my life can eventually create a larger structure. This network of mutual connections, created by ambitious, innovative entrepreneurs who want to broaden their horizons and gain more experience, inspires me to act. The idea arose as a result of the growing interest of Turkish students and teachers in our country. As a new entity on the market, created by young, ambitious and freshly perceived entrepreneurs, our task is to offer services that are innovative and at the highest level. “No pain, no gain”
