Our Mission

GALILEO LIFELONG LEARNING CENTER

Offers numerous development opportunities for students, university employees and many other groups from around the world, who dream of science beyond their country.

Training system abroad, in addition to increasing professional perspectives and experience, gives the opportunity to get independence, get to know new cultures and improve foreign languages. University and Vocational school graduates may count for help in finding a job, internship or application to universities in Poland. Employees employed in the education sector have the possibility of conducting lectures in foreign facilities, sharing their knowledge, participating in research and finding news.
We organise :

  • Internships
  • Training
  • Sport, recreational and science camps
  • Conferences
  • Stationary and online courses
  • Excursions
  • Finding accommodations
  • Helping during process for start studying in Poland
