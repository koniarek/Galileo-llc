About Us

GALILEO LIFELONG LEARNING CENTER

Galileo Lifelong Learning Centre plays an active role in developing the Erasmus projectsâ€™ overall strategy and in designing and implementing education policies. GLL Centre is open to many individuals and organisations, although eligibility varies from one action to another and from one country to another. However, it provides opportunities almost for everyone in the field of education; teachers, trainers, students, doctors, professors, universities and businesses.

The aim of GLL Centre is to contribute to the Europe 2020 strategy for growth, jobs, social equity and inclusion, as well as the aims of ET2020, the EU's strategic framework for education and training. We organise :

  • Internships
  • Training
  • Encouraging young people to take part in European democracy
  • Supporting innovation, cooperation and reform
  • Reducing unemployment, especially among young people
  • Promoting adult learning, especially for new skills and skills required by the labour market.
  • Reducing early school leaving
  • Promoting cooperation and mobility with the EU's partner countries

The Galileo Lifelong Learning Center offers numerous development opportunities for many other groups from around the world who dream of learning outside their home country. The training system abroad, in addition to increasing career prospects and experience in the field of mobile education, gives opportunities to gain independence, learn about new cultures and improve foreign languages.

Graduates of vocational schools and universities can count on help in, internship or apprenticeship in the European Union, especially in Poland. Employees employed in the education sector have the opportunity to give lectures in foreign institutions, share expertise in their field, participate in research and make new friends.
